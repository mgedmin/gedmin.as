Spring 2004

ACM organizes an annual programming contest for students called ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest).

Vilnius University teams participate in ICPC since 2000.

I was leading a training seminar in spring of 2004. Its purpose was to prepare Vilnius University teams for the upcoming competition.

You can find out more if you go to the Lithuanian version of this page.
